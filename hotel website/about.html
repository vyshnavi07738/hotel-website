About Royal Palace Hotel

Who We Are

Royal Palace Hotel is a luxury hotel in Chennai that offers comfortable rooms, modern facilities, delicious dining and excellent hospitality. Our hotel is designed for families, tourists and business travelers who want a peaceful and memorable stay.

We provide premium rooms, affordable room prices, swimming pool, gym, restaurant, free Wi-Fi, parking and easy online room booking. Our main goal is to make every guest feel safe, comfortable and satisfied.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide world-class hospitality with comfort, cleanliness, safety and affordable pricing. We focus on quality service and guest happiness.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted hotels in Chennai by offering excellent service, modern facilities and unforgettable guest experiences.
