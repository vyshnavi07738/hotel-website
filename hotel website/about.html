About Royal Palace Hotel – Luxury Hotel in Chennai

Luxury • Comfort • Hospitality • Premium Guest Experience

Experience World-Class Hospitality in Chennai

Royal Palace Hotel is one of the leading luxury hotels in Chennai, offering elegant accommodation, premium hotel rooms, affordable room prices and outstanding hospitality. Our hotel is designed to provide comfort, convenience and memorable experiences for families, tourists, business travellers and couples seeking a relaxing stay.

We offer Deluxe Rooms, Premium Rooms and Luxury Suite Rooms equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning, free high-speed Wi-Fi, LED televisions, comfortable bedding and excellent room service. Guests can also enjoy our swimming pool, fitness centre, multi-cuisine restaurant, conference facilities and secure online room booking.

At Royal Palace Hotel, our mission is to combine luxury accommodation with exceptional customer service, creating unforgettable experiences for every guest. Whether you are visiting Chennai for business, leisure or a family vacation, our dedicated hospitality team ensures every stay is comfortable, safe and enjoyable.
